## Overview
This project presents a T-SQL exploration of sales, products, factories, customers & candy for a fictious US Candy Distributor where we have been tasked in discovering reasons for their sales decline since 2023


---

## Brief Description
The US Candy Distributor has factories across the US which sell to candy vendors. The database stores sales and inventory for the factories as well as product information about the various candies available.

---

## Objectives
- Which candy division drives the most revenue?
- Which products are most and least profitable?
- Are divisions hitting their monthly sales targets?
- Which customer types spend the most?
- Which products are the slowest movers?
- Which factories generate the highest gross profit?
